Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore

is a fictional character in J. K. Rowling's Harry Potter series.
For most of the series, he is the headmaster of the wizarding school Hogwarts.
As part of his backstory, it is revealed that he is the founder and leader of
the Order of the Phoenix, an organisation dedicated to fighting Lord
Voldemort, the main antagonist of the series
